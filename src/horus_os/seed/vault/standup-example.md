# Standup (Example)

A quick daily status, whether you work solo or on a team. Three questions,
short answers. Copy it each day or keep a rolling log.

## Date

### Yesterday

- What you finished or moved forward.

### Today

- What you plan to focus on.

### Blockers

- Anything in your way, and who or what could unblock it. Write "none" if
  there are none.

Optional context that does not fit the three questions above.
