# Project Ideas

A running list of things worth building someday. Add freely; prune later. An
idea here is not a commitment, just a place to park a thought.

## Active candidates

- Idea: a one-line pitch of what it is and who it helps.
  - Why now: what makes this worth doing.
  - First step: the smallest thing that would prove it out.

## Someday / maybe

- A second idea, with just enough detail to remember what you meant.
- A third idea, parked for later.

## Parking lot

Half-formed thoughts that are not ready to be ideas yet. Move them up when
they sharpen.
