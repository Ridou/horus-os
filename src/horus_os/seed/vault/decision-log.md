# Decision Log

A record of decisions worth remembering, so you do not relitigate them later.
Add an entry whenever you make a choice you might second-guess.

## How to use this

Each entry captures what you decided, when, why, and what you considered. The
point is to remember the reasoning, not just the outcome.

## Entries

### Decision: short title

- Date:
- Decision: what you chose.
- Context: the situation that forced the choice.
- Options considered: the alternatives and why they lost.
- Consequences: what this commits you to.
- Revisit when: the signal that would make you reopen this.
